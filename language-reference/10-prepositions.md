# 10 — Prepositions and Spatial Language

> Prepositions are the glue between nouns, verbs, and meaning.
> Master 20 prepositions and you can describe where, when, why, and how anything happens.

---

## The Big 10 Prepositions

### 1. **a** — to, at

The most versatile preposition. Four core uses:

**Direction / destination:**
- *Voy **a** la tienda.* — I'm going to the store.
- *Llegamos **a** las tres.* — We arrive at three.

**Indirect object marker:**
- *Le doy el libro **a** María.* — I give the book to María.
- *Dile **a** tu mamá.* — Tell your mom.

**Personal `a`** (required before human direct objects):
- *Veo **a** mi amigo.* — I see my friend.
- *Llamo **a** mi hermana.* — I call my sister.
- *Busco **a** alguien.* — I'm looking for someone.
- But NOT with things: *Veo la casa.* (I see the house — no `a`)

**Time expressions:**
- *a las ocho* — at eight o'clock
- *a medianoche* — at midnight
- *a veces* — sometimes

---

### 2. **de** — of, from, about

**Origin:**
- *Soy **de** Nevada.* — I'm from Nevada.
- *Vengo **de** la oficina.* — I'm coming from the office.

**Possession (replaces English 's):**
- *el libro **de** María* — María's book
- *la casa **de** mis padres* — my parents' house

**Material / content:**
- *una mesa **de** madera* — a wooden table
- *un vaso **de** agua* — a glass of water

**About / concerning:**
- *Hablamos **de** todo.* — We talked about everything.
- *¿**De** qué se trata?* — What's it about?

**Contraction:** de + el = **del**
- *Vengo **del** trabajo.* — I'm coming from work.

---

### 3. **en** — in, on, at (location)

**Location (static):**
- *Estoy **en** casa.* — I'm at home.
- *El libro está **en** la mesa.* — The book is on the table.
- *Vivo **en** Nevada.* — I live in Nevada.

**Transportation:**
- *Voy **en** carro.* — I go by car.
- *Viajo **en** avión.* — I travel by plane.
- Exception: *a pie* (on foot) — uses `a`, not `en`

**Time (months, years, seasons):**
- *en marzo* — in March
- *en 2026* — in 2026
- *en verano* — in summer

**Key difference from `a`:** `en` = static location (being somewhere); `a` = movement toward.
- *Estoy **en** la tienda.* — I'm at the store. (already there)
- *Voy **a** la tienda.* — I'm going to the store. (moving toward)

---

### 4. **con** — with

**Accompaniment:**
- *Voy **con** mi esposa.* — I'm going with my wife.
- *Café **con** leche.* — Coffee with milk.

**Instrument / means:**
- *Corto **con** tijeras.* — I cut with scissors.
- *Escribo **con** lápiz.* — I write with pencil.

**Special contractions:**
- con + mí = **conmigo** (with me)
- con + ti = **contigo** (with you)
- con + él/ella = **con él / con ella** (no contraction)

---

### 5. **sin** — without

- *Café **sin** azúcar.* — Coffee without sugar.
- *Salió **sin** decir nada.* — He left without saying anything.
- ***Sin** problema.* — No problem.
- *Estoy **sin** dinero.* — I'm out of money.

---

### 6. **para** — for (purpose, destination, deadline)

See the [Por vs Para deep dive](#por-vs-para-the-deep-dive) below.

**Quick version — PARA answers:** For whom? By when? What's the purpose? Where's it heading?

- *Esto es **para** ti.* — This is for you.
- *Es **para** mañana.* — It's for tomorrow (deadline).
- *Estudio **para** aprender.* — I study in order to learn.
- *Salgo **para** Madrid.* — I'm leaving for Madrid (destination).

---

### 7. **por** — for (cause, exchange, duration, through)

See the [Por vs Para deep dive](#por-vs-para-the-deep-dive) below.

**Quick version — POR answers:** Why? (cause) How long? Through where? In exchange for what?

- *Gracias **por** todo.* — Thanks for everything (cause).
- *Estuve allí **por** tres horas.* — I was there for three hours (duration).
- *Paso **por** tu casa.* — I'll pass by your house (through).
- *Lo compré **por** diez dólares.* — I bought it for ten dollars (exchange).

---

### 8. **sobre** — on, on top of, about

**Physical position:**
- *El libro está **sobre** la mesa.* — The book is on the table.

**About / regarding:**
- *Hablamos **sobre** el proyecto.* — We talked about the project.

**Approximate time:**
- ***Sobre** las tres.* — Around three o'clock.

---

### 9. **entre** — between, among

- ***Entre** tú y yo...* — Between you and me...
- *La tienda está **entre** el banco y la farmacia.* — The store is between the bank and the pharmacy.
- ***Entre** semana.* — During the week (weekdays).
- ***Entre** todos lo hicimos.* — We all did it together (among all of us).

---

### 10. **hasta** — until, up to, even

**Time limit:**
- *Trabajo **hasta** las cinco.* — I work until five.
- ***Hasta** mañana.* — See you tomorrow (until tomorrow).
- ***Hasta** luego.* — See you later.

**Spatial limit:**
- *Caminé **hasta** la esquina.* — I walked up to / as far as the corner.

**"Even" (emphatic):**
- ***Hasta** mi mamá lo sabe.* — Even my mom knows it.

## The Next 10 Prepositions

### 11. **desde** — from, since

- *Trabajo aquí **desde** 2020.* — I've worked here since 2020.
- ***Desde** mi casa hasta la oficina.* — From my house to the office.
- ***Desde** entonces...* — Since then...

**desde + hasta = from...to:**
- ***Desde** las ocho **hasta** las cinco.* — From eight to five.

### 12. **hacia** — toward(s)

- *Camino **hacia** la playa.* — I walk toward the beach.
- ***Hacia** el norte.* — Toward the north.
- ***Hacia** las tres.* — Around three o'clock.

### 13. **durante** — during, for (duration)

- ***Durante** la reunión.* — During the meeting.
- *Viví allí **durante** cinco años.* — I lived there for five years.

### 14. **según** — according to

- ***Según** mi mamá...* — According to my mom...
- ***Según** las noticias...* — According to the news...

### 15. **contra** — against

- *Jugamos **contra** ellos.* — We play against them.
- *Estoy **contra** esa idea.* — I'm against that idea.

### 16. **tras** — after, behind (formal/literary)

- ***Tras** la tormenta viene la calma.* — After the storm comes the calm.
- More common in speech: *después de* (after)

### 17. **ante** — before, in the presence of (formal)

- ***Ante** todo, calm.* — Above all, calm.
- More common in speech: *antes de* (before) or *frente a* (in front of / facing)

### 18. **bajo** — under, below

- ***Bajo** la mesa.* — Under the table.
- ***Bajo** presión.* — Under pressure.
- More common in speech: *debajo de* (underneath)

### 19. **excepto / salvo** — except

- *Todos vinieron **excepto** Juan.* — Everyone came except Juan.
- *Todo bien, **salvo** un problema.* — All good, except one problem.

### 20. **mediante** — by means of (formal)

- *Lo logré **mediante** mucho esfuerzo.* — I achieved it through a lot of effort.
- More common in speech: *por medio de* or *a través de*

## Por vs Para — The Deep Dive

This is the #1 confusion point for English speakers because both translate to "for." Here's how to tell them apart.

### The Mental Model

**PARA** = forward-looking: destination, purpose, deadline, recipient
**POR** = backward-looking: cause, exchange, duration, path

Think of it as:
- **PARA** = Where is it going? Why? (future / intention)
- **POR** = Where did it come from? How? (past / cause)

### PARA Uses (Purpose, Destination, Deadline, Recipient)

| Use | Example | Translation |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| **Purpose** | Estudio para aprender. | I study to learn. |
| **Recipient** | Este regalo es para ti. | This gift is for you. |
| **Destination** | Salgo para Madrid. | I'm leaving for Madrid. |
| **Deadline** | Es para el lunes. | It's for (due) Monday. |
| **Comparison** | Para un niño, habla bien. | For a child, he speaks well. |
| **Opinion** | Para mí, es fácil. | For me / In my opinion, it's easy. |
| **Employment** | Trabajo para Google. | I work for Google. |
| **"In order to"** | Vine para ayudarte. | I came (in order) to help you. |

**Memory aid:** PARA = **P**urpose, **A**iming at, **R**ecipient, **A**head (deadline)

### POR Uses (Cause, Exchange, Duration, Through)

| Use | Example | Translation |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| **Cause / reason** | Gracias por tu ayuda. | Thanks for your help. |
| **Exchange** | Pagué diez dólares por esto. | I paid $10 for this. |
| **Duration** | Viví allí por tres años. | I lived there for 3 years. |
| **Through / along** | Caminé por el parque. | I walked through the park. |
| **By means of** | Hablamos por teléfono. | We talked by phone. |
| **On behalf of** | Lo hice por ti. | I did it for you (for your sake). |
| **Per** | Ganamos por uno. | We won by one. |
| **Approximate time** | Llega por la mañana. | He arrives in the morning. |
| **Passive "by"** | Fue escrito por Cervantes. | It was written by Cervantes. |

**Memory aid:** POR = **P**ath, **O**rigin/cause, **R**eason/exchange

### Decision Tree

```
Is the sentence about...

PURPOSE / GOAL?      → para  (Estudio para aprender)
RECIPIENT?           → para  (Es para ti)
DESTINATION?         → para  (Salgo para Madrid)
DEADLINE?            → para  (Es para el viernes)

CAUSE / REASON?      → por   (Gracias por todo)
EXCHANGE?            → por   (Lo cambié por otro)
DURATION?            → por   (Viví allí por un año)
THROUGH / ALONG?     → por   (Pasé por tu casa)
BY MEANS OF?         → por   (Lo mandé por correo)
```

### The Tricky Pairs

| English | Por | Para |
|---------|-----|------|
| I did it for you | Lo hice **por** ti. (for your sake / because of you) | Lo hice **para** ti. (as a gift for you) |
| I work for Google | — | Trabajo **para** Google. (employed by) |
| Thanks for the food | Gracias **por** la comida. (because of) | — |
| She left for Madrid | — | Salió **para** Madrid. (heading to) |
| He walked for an hour | Caminó **por** una hora. (duration) | — |
| This medicine is for pain | — | Esta medicina es **para** el dolor. (purpose) |

### Fixed Expressions with Por

| Expression | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| por favor | please |
| por supuesto | of course |
| por eso | that's why / therefore |
| por fin | finally |
| por lo menos | at least |
| por ejemplo | for example |
| por cierto | by the way |
| por lo general | generally |
| por primera vez | for the first time |
| por otro lado | on the other hand |
| por si acaso | just in case |
| ¿por qué? | why? |
| porque | because |

### Fixed Expressions with Para

| Expression | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| para siempre | forever |
| para nada | not at all |
| para colmo | to top it off |
| para entonces | by then |
| para que | so that (+ subjunctive) |
| para empezar | to begin with |
| ¿para qué? | what for? |
| no es para tanto | it's not such a big deal |

## Verb + Preposition Combos

Many Spanish verbs require specific prepositions that don't match English. These must be memorized as units:

| ayudar a | to help to |
| enseñar a | to teach to |
| invitar a | to invite to |
| llegar a | to arrive at |
| volver a | to do again |

### Verb + de

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|
| dejar de | to stop (doing) |

| acabar de | to have just (done) |
| olvidarse de | to forget about |
| depender de | to depend on |
| disfrutar de | to enjoy |

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|

### Verb + con

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|
| soñar con | to dream about |

| Spanish | English |
