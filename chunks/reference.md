# Chunk Reference

Production-first phrases organized by world. These are the universal building blocks.

This is the **engine reference** — every chunk you'll ever need, organized by world and use case.
Track your personal mastery state in your learner data directory at `progress/chunks.md`.

**How to use this file:**
1. Before each session: review the section for today's world
2. Use your `progress/chunks.md` to track which ones you've mastered (SRS Box 4)
3. After sessions: add new chunks discovered during friction to your personal progress file

### TV & Entertainment (Bridgerton / Ninjago) 🆕

Connection through shared shows — works with neighbor adults (Bridgerton) and kids (Ninjago).

| Chunk | English | Note |
|-------|---------|------|
| ¿Viste Bridgerton? | Did you see Bridgerton? | Past tense opener |
| ¿Viste la nueva temporada? | Did you see the new season? | Temporada = TV season |
| Estoy viendo ___ ahorita | I'm watching ___ right now | Estoy + viendo (-iendo form) |
| Me encanta | I love it | Strong opinion, natural |
| Está bueno | It's good | Medium opinion |
| Está bien | It's okay | Mild |
| ¿Cuál es tu personaje favorito? | What's your favorite character? | Cuál not Qué for favorites |
| ¡Es el mejor! | He's the best! | Kid energy |
| Mi favorito es ___ | My favorite is ___ | Drop any name |
| ¡Está chido! | He's/It's cool! | Mexican slang — kids love it |
| A mí me gusta más ___ | I like ___ more | Comparing favorites |
| Lo vemos en familia | We watch it as a family | nosotros vemos → lo vemos |
| Se llama ___ | His/her name is ___ | Introducing someone |
| A él también le encanta ___ | He also loves ___ | también = too/also |
| ¡El ninja verde! | The green ninja! | Verde — NOT vert (French!) |
| Es para bebés | It's for babies | bebés = beh-BAYS |
| No, son muy pequeños | No, they're too little | muy = very, muchos = many |
| Yo no vi todavía | I haven't seen it yet | todavía = yet/still |
| ¡Fue un gusto! | It was nice (meeting/talking)! | Warm conversation close |
| ¡Igualmente! | Likewise! / Same to you! | Universal warm response |

**Key vocab:** personaje (character) · temporada (season) · viendo (watching) · favorito (follows noun) · también (too/also) · pequeños (little/young) · todavía (yet/still)

**⚠️ French shelf warning:** *vert→verde*, *non→no*, *saison→temporada* — slow down on color/season words

**⚠️ Verb forms — ver (to watch/see):**
- yo veo · tú ves · él/ella ve · nosotros vemos · ellos ven
- present continuous: estoy viendo (I'm watching)
- simple past: vi (I saw), viste (you saw)
